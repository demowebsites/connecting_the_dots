About SB Contest

An incredible chance for all the WIE AG Student Chapters to learn more about other WIE Student Chapters Activities, and to meet Influential Mentors and top-notch Students from Various Branches.

Eligibility

  • Should hold Women in Engineering Student Branch Affinity group of IEEE Madras Section.
  • The statistics of AG shall be required to maintain a membership of not fewer than twenty-five (25) IEEE WIE members.
  • The AG shall be required to hold not less than five (5) meetings per year.
  • The AG Secretary/Treasurer shall be required to submit an annual report of meetings, activities and the roster of Exe-com members to Madras section WIE unit.

Affinity Groups are required to structure out the initiatives that:-

  • Facilitated the development of programs and activities that promote the entry into and retention of members especially women in WIE programs.
  • Obtained financial support for activities from the Section, comprising the funds from IEEE WIE or from income producing activities, such as symposium and conferences, sponsored by the Council, or as otherwise provided by the Region.
  • Received the rebate funds from the section allowed for Society Chapters or Affinity Groups activities. If a Council/Region is a sponsoring unit of an Affinity Group, shall be required to submit the meeting and officer reports of those units in an annual year.
  • AGs those who have applied or received any WIE Awards in the past, shall be required to mention.
  • Success metrics of the activities shall be required to present through the impacts it has created by providing Internships/Jobs/Projects related opportunities.

Rules for participation Prior to the Contest:-

  • WiE AGs who are willing to take part should get registered through our website.
  • The registration will be taken as a valid entry only if it is made by the following members:-
    • Student Branch Chairperson.
    • WiE Chairperson of the SB.
  • Registered team will be receiving approval mail after proper validation from the organising team and only those teams alone will be proceeding for the contest round.
  • The PPT has to be submitted to the organising team at the mentioned time in the prescribed format. [Format of PPT and mode of Submission will be intimated via mail to the approved team]

During the Contest:-

  • The PPT presentation in the prescribed format will alone be considered for presentation.
  • The WiE AG Chairperson of the SB or, in the event of his/her unavailability, the WiE AG Vice-Chairperson of the SB/ SB Chairperson, shall be responsible for presentation.
  • A quorum for a contest presentation shall consist of Exe-com members of the SB and shall include Advisor of AG/ Counsellor of SB/ Exe-com members of the WiE AG of the SB.

  • Timings:-

    • 7 minutes totally per team
      • 5 minutes for Presentation
      • 2 minutes for Q&A

Important Dates:-

  • Registration Starts: November 16
  • Registration Ends: November 20
  • Final Presentation Submission: November 24
  • Contest Date: Nov28
