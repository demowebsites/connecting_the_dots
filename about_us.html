About Connecting The Dots

IEEE Madras Section Women in Engineering is organizing 2 days sessions on “Connecting the dots with IEEE Madras WIE” to create the awareness about WIE activities, Women in technology, Leadership roles, etc. to bring a lot of women leaders as role models who can inspire the future generation of WIE. These sessions and contest are intended to provide an opportunity to the student’s members and WIE Members, to interact with the IEEE Madras WIE Committee members along with WIE Members from all the WIE Chapters across IEEE Madras Section who are the live examples of leaders. The event is planned to be conducted on November 27 and 28 from 05:00 PM – 08:00 PM (3 hours/day) through virtual call. The link to the sessions will be shared to the attendees who registered.

Vision

IEEE Madras Section WIE’S “Connecting the Dots” visions to make women and members of IEEE WIE aware of the vast repository of resources across IEEE available to uplift and help their professional and personal development. The core vision is to encourage more Women to get involved with the latest technologies and to create awareness about WIE activities and Leadership Roles.

Mission

Spread the visibility of IEEE WIE Chapters across IEEE Madras Section. Create opportunity for students and WIE members to interact with Women leaders. Motivate Women members across Madras Section to apply for awards/events.
